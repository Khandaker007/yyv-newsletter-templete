Orange Corners Bangladesh Incubation Programme

Orange Corners Bangladesh Incubation programme is designed for aspiring Bangladeshi youth with sustainable business solutions to solve local challenges and contribute to the Sustainable Development Goals. In May 2023, YY Ventures launched the applications for the first cohort of the Incubation Programme. After a month-long campaign, we received a total of 229 applications. The top 33 teams were invited into a 2-day selection boot camp on June 25-26, 2023, at the Orange Coners space at Impact Hub Dhaka. Day 01 of the boot camp consisted of capacity-building workshops and an orientation to funding opportunities available through the Orange Corners Bangladesh programme designed to help them prepare a business pitch. The participants pitched their business ideas in two separate pitching sessions, ran simultaneously in 15-minuteslots throughouttheday, competing for a spot in Cohort 1. After the pitching session, 15 brilliant teams were onboarded into the Incubation Programme. To learn more about these teams, click here.

Social Fiction Design Competition

The Social Fiction Design Competition invited visionaries and dreamers all over the world with a knack for creative writing to share how they wish to see the world and the lives of young people in the year 2050, with a chance to win USD 10,000. This served as a global platform encouraging Social Fiction to imagine a world we want to live in, a world free of social and environmental injustices that plague our communities. After receiving 656 applications from across 70+ countries, the top 12 teams were invited for a workshop on ‘Crafting Your Story’ on June 13, 2023, and the participants then got an opportunity to showcase their stories at the final story telling session on June 23, 2023.
